About ibscienceclassroom

Empowering IB educators with high-quality, curriculum-aligned resources to enhance teaching and student learning outcomes.

Mission Statement

This curriculum extends beyond the classroom and is aligned with IB science assessment objectives, modern literacy, Approaches to Learning (ATL) skills, and scientific knowledge. It promotes interdisciplinary learning, enabling students to transfer their understanding and apply knowledge in real-life situations. As a result, it empowers students to become lifelong learners and global citizens who take action to address real-world problems.

Teaching style

My teaching philosophy is grounded in the belief that students construct their own understanding through inquiry, enabling them to become confident, independent, and lifelong learners. I teach through concepts, which allows students to make interdisciplinary connections and see the bigger picture. Each resource is carefully designed to:

  • Align perfectly with IB standards and assessment criteria
  • Promote inquiry-based learning and critical thinking
  • Save teachers hours of preparation time
  • Engage students with interactive, visually appealing materials

I believe that learning happens through doing. My classroom is an active space where students learn best by engaging hands-on, exploring ideas, and experiencing science in action. This approach fosters critical thinking and encourages international mindedness, both essential elements of the IB framework. From complete unit bundles to individual lesson plans, I share resources designed to inspire inquiry-based learning, support conceptual understanding, and empower both teachers and students.
