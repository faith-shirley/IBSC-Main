Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and for school plans, we can also accept purchase orders and bank transfers.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly or annual subscription at any time. For annual subscriptions, we offer a prorated refund for unused months if canceled within the first 30 days.

Do you offer discounts for IB schools in developing countries?

Yes, we offer special pricing for schools in developing countries. Please contact us with your school details, and we'll provide customized pricing options.

Can I share resources with colleagues?

Individual and subscription plans are for single-teacher use only. For sharing with colleagues, please consider our department or school-wide plans which are designed for multiple users.

How often are new resources added?

We add new resources weekly, with major updates aligned with the IB academic calendar. Subscribers receive notifications when new resources matching their subjects are added.

Not sure which plan is right for you?

Our team is happy to help you choose the best option for your teaching needs and budget.
